Research Focus
My research explores molecular electronic junctions — the charge transport phenomena in metal|molecule|metal assemblies. I employ EGaIn large-area junctions and photocurrent spectroscopy to probe quantum mechanical phenomena at the nanoscale. My work bridges fundamental physics with real-world applications in sensing, supercapacitors, and molecular electronics, published in journals including JACS (IF 15.6), ACS Sensors (IF 9.1), and Nanoscale.
