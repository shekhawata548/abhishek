Research Focus
My research explores molecular electronic junctions — particularly the charge transport phenomena in metal|molecule|metal assemblies. I employ EGaIn large-area junctions and photocurrent spectroscopy to probe quantum mechanical phenomena at the nanoscale. My work bridges fundamental physics with applications in sensing, supercapacitors, and molecular electronics, with publications in journals including JACS, ACS Sensors, and Nanoscale.
